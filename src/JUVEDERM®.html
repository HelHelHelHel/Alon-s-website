Dermal fillers are compounds containing hyaluronic acid, a natural compound that restores volume and fullness to your face and also diminishes facial wrinkles.

Hyaluronic acid attracts water and maintains your skin’s water volume so you achieve the look of greater hydration and fullness almost immediately.

You might seek treatment with dermal fillers to:

  • Plump your lips
  • Soften creases, folds, and wrinkles around your eyes, nose, and mouth
  • Add volume to hollowed cheeks
  • Lift sagging skin
  • Reduce the look of aging under the eyes

Natural loss of subcutaneous facial fat occurs as you age. As you lose this fat, facial muscles become closer to the skin's surface, making wrinkles and expression lines stand out more. Dermal fillers address exactly that, feeling the naturally created voids.

Injectable aesthetics fillers can help provide a more youthful look for a fraction of what a traditional facelift costs. These fillers add volume and fill lines and wrinkles in less than 30 minutes. fillers fill the lines, creases, and volume loss areas with one of several different substances. As a result, trouble spots nearly disappear. Aesthetic fillers are used as “volumizers,” plumping and lifting cheeks, jawlines, and temples; filling out thin lips, and plumping sagging hands. The treatment is fast and easy.

Hyaluronic acid is a key ingredient in a family of injectable fillers that has revolutionized the in-office treatment of deeper wrinkles. A variety of products are available (JUVÉDERM, Stylage, Restylane, Revanesse and Neauvia), each with its own advantages.
